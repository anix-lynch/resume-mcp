To get your static domain:

1. Go to: https://dashboard.ngrok.com
2. Click on "Domains" in the left sidebar
3. You should see your reserved domain listed
4. It will look like: yourname.ngrok-free.app

OR if you haven't reserved one yet:
1. Click "Reserve Domain" or "Add Domain"
2. Choose a name (e.g., "anix-resume")
3. Your domain will be: anix-resume.ngrok-free.app
